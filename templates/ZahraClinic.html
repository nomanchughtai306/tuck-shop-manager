Meet Dr. Ruqayya

"Every woman deserves to be heard, respected, and treated with the highest caliber of medical expertise."

As the founder and lead practitioner at Zahra Clinic, Dr. Ruqayya brings years of intensive experience in obstetrics and gynecology. Her approach to medicine is deeply holistic—combining rigorous clinical protocols with a comforting, personalized bedside manner.

Understanding that women's health issues can be sensitive, Dr. Ruqayya ensures that consultations are never rushed. She takes the time necessary to explain diagnoses, outline options, and partner with patients to make informed health decisions.
